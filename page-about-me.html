Ivi Sims

CEO

Professional Profile- Ivi Sims, CEO Flood Restoration and Structural Drying Australia Pty Ltd. (FRDSA)

Ivi Sims has been working in the cleaning and restoration industry for the last 15 years. Previous to that she was working as a Project Manager for various media groups.

Ivi Sims is one of the pioneers in eco or green cleaning implementing many green programs for commercial and residential clients in Melbourne going back to 2006.

For the last 5 years, Ivi has acquired designations with the Institute of Inspection Cleaning and Restoration (IICRC). She holds certificates as Master in Fire and Smoke; Master in Textile Technician and Journeyman in Water Restorer. In April Ivi will travel to USA to gain her final designation as Master Water Restorer. With these Master certificates, Ivi will join an elite group who hold internationally recognized designations.

Ivi’s aim is to grow FRDSA into a nationally recognized business that helps the building industry, restoration industry, homeowners and insurers attain the highest results in specialist drying.

FRSDA is actively involved in association such as Restoration Industry Association (RIA), Specialist Cleaning and Restoration Industry Association (SCRIA), Australian Timber Flooring Association (ATFA) and joined Woolsafe organisation in 2014.
